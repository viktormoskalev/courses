The remaining columns specify the maximum tolerance and clearance allowed for by an LC1 fit. For instance, the maximum clearance value is .0009" (.9 × .001). Similarly, the hole diameter can vary from nominal .750 (–0) to .7505 (.750 +.5 × .001), and the shaft can vary in diameter from .750 (+0) to .7496 (.750–.4 × .001).
